Crochet Heart Coaster

Would you like to deocrate mundane objects 2 beautifully? You can do so with these crochet heart coasters!





Materials needed

for this pattern you will need:


  • ( Milk Cotton Yarn, 5ply )
  • Rose pink yarn
  • White yarn
  • 3mm Hook Size
  • scissors
  • Needle




Abbreviations

*All patterns are written in standard US terms *

Stitches used in the pattern:


  • Ch – chain 3
  • Hdc – half double crochet
  • Sc – single crochet
  • Sl st – slip stitch




Written Pattern


Round 1:
make a slip knot.
chain 8
skip 2 chain
make 3 Dc into the next 2 stitch
make 1 Dc
make 6 Dc into the last stitch. ( Work on the other side of the chain )
Join 4 Dc together over the next 4 chain
Slip stitch into the beginning Dc

Round 2:
Chain 2
make 1 Dc into the next 4 stitch
make 3 Dc into the same stitch
make 1 Dc into the next 4 stitch again
make 2 Dc into the next 5 stitch
make 1 HDC 1 SC 1 HDC
make 2 Dc into the next 5 stitch again
slip stitch

Round 3 :
chain 2
make 1 Dc into the next 5 stitch
make 3 Dc into the same stitch
make 1 Dc into the next 5 stitch again
make 2 Dc into the next 8 stitch
make 1 DC, 1 DC, 1 HDC, 1 SC, 1 HDC, 1 DC, 1 DC
make 2 Dc into the next 8 stitch again
Slip stitch

Ruffle Trim
make a slip knot with the white yarn
attach the white yarn into the middle of SC
chain 4
skip 2 chain
slip stitch
chain 4
skip 1 chain
slip stitch
( slip stitch back into the sc for the last chain )
