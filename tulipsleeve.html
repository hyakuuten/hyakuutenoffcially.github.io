Crochet Tulips Book Sleeve

Everyone has their favorite book which they would protect at all costs.In that case, crochet comes in handy. Your book gets carried in a secure spring seasoned tulip sleeve. No need to wait for the arrival of spring when you can hold it!





Materials needed

for this pattern you will need:


  • white Yarn
  • Green yarn
  • Pink yarn
  • 2.50mm Hook
  • Scissors
  • Needle
  • Book




Abbreviations

*All patterns are written in standard US terms *

Stitches used in the pattern:


  • Ch – chain
  • Hdc – half double crochet
  • Sc – single crochet
  • Sl st – slip stitch
  • v-st – dc, ch 1, dc in same st.
  • long hdc – hdc in the spaces created between v-sts from rnd 4.
  • Popcorn Stitch (Pop-St) – 5 dc in same st, on last stitch draw up a 1″ loop, remove hook, insert hook beside first dc in behind the 5 dc, attach loop onto hook and draw up a loop.




Written Pattern


STEP 1 :
Take measurements of your selected book,just in case, to determine the number of chains of your foundation chain. This patterns fits the standard book size.
Round 1 :
Ch 60, making sure not to twist work, join with sl st.

Round 2:
ch 1, sc in same st and each st around, join with sl st to beg sc. (60 sc)

Round 3:
rep rnd 2, when joining with sl st draw up green coloured yarn. .

Round 4:
ch 4, dc in same st as beg ch4 (this is your first v-st), *sk 2 sts, v-st in next st* 19 times, fasten off, join with an invisible join to the 3rd ch of beg ch4, weave in ends. (20 v-sts)

Round 5:
Using rose coloured yarn, insert hook into one of the v-sts from rnd 4, draw up a loop, ch 2, 5 dc in the same v-st as beg ch2, draw up a 1″ loop, remove hook and insert hook between ch2 and first dc, insert hook into loop and draw up a loop, *ch 3, pop-st in next v-st* 19 times, after last pop-st, ch 3, join in 2nd ch of beg ch2 drawing up black coloured yarn. (20 pop-sts)
Round 6:
ch 1, sc in same st and each st around, join with sl st to first sc. (60 sc)

Round 7:

rep rnd 6

Round 8:
rep rnd 7, do not join, fasten off, invisible join.
