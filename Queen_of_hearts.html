Crochet Queen of Hearts Bag

Are you always on the look for cute bags,but never suceeded? Well the wait is over, This "Queen of Hearts" inspired bag is the cutest you'll ever find or make.





Materials needed

for this pattern you will need:


  • Red Yarn
  • Whute Yarn
  • Hook
  • Scissors




Abbreviations

*All patterns are written in standard US terms *

Stitches used in the pattern:


  • Dc - double crochet
  • Hdc – half double crochet
  • Sc – single crochet
  • Sl st – slip stitch




Written Pattern


STEP 1

Make 8 granny squares

Round 1:
Ch 4, sl st to first ch to form ring
Ch 5 (counts as dc and ch 2), [3dc into ring, ch 2] 3 times. Ch 2, 2dc into ring, sl st to 3rd ch from beg chain. (12dc total; 3dc each side)

Round 2:
Sl st to ch2 sp, Ch 5, 2dc into same sp. *Dc in each of the next 3 st, [2dc, ch 2, 2dc] in corner ch2 sp. Rep * until you reach the last side. Dc in each of the next 3 st, dc in first space where you created your ch 5. Sl st to 3rd ch. (28dc total; 7dc each side)

Round 3:
Sl st to ch2 sp, Ch 5, 2dc into same sp. *Dc in each of the next 7 st, [2dc, ch 2, 2dc] in corner ch2 sp. Rep * until you reach the last side. Dc in each of the next 7 st, dc in first space where you created your ch 5. Sl st to 3rd ch. (44dc total; 11dc each side)

Round 4:
- Sl st to ch2 sp, Ch 5, 2dc into same sp. *Dc in each of the next 11 st, [2dc, ch 2, 2dc] in corner ch2 sp. Rep * until you reach the last side. Dc in each of the next 11 st, dc in first space where you created your ch 5. Sl st to 3rd ch. (60dc total; 15dc each side)
sl st into next space and tie off your work.


STEP 2

Make 10 Heart Granny Squares

Heart Motif
Round 1:
: Start with a magic ring in 1stcolour (pink). Ch 2 (counts as one dc), 2 dc into the ring, ch 2. *3 dc, ch 2, rep from * 2 more times. Tighten the magic loop and join rnd with sl st to first st. You should have 4 sections of 3 dc and ch 2 joined in a square here.

Round 2:
Sl st into next dc, ch 1, make 8 dc into the first gap (which was a ch 2 in the previous rnd.) Ch 1, sc into next ch 2 gap. Ch 1, skip the first dc (of the 3 dc section). In the second dc, make 2 sc, ch 1 and 2 sc all in the same st. Ch 1, sc into the ch 2 gap. Ch 1, 8 dc in the final ch 2 gap. Ch 1 and sl st into the second dc to join and finish the rnd.
CH1,cut off yarn, fasten off and weave in the ends. (Make sure the center loop is tight so the heart looks neat!)

Granny Square
Round 1:
: Start with a magic ring in 1stcolour (pink). Ch 2 (counts as one dc), 2 dc into the ring, ch 2. *3 dc, ch 2, rep from * 2 more times. Tighten the magic loop and join rnd with sl st to first st. You should have 4 sections of 3 dc and ch 2 joined in a square here.

Round 2:
Insert hook into back loop of the bottom point st of the heart. Sl st with 2nd colour (white) and ch 1. Sc, hdc, and dc. Ch 2 to create a corner, then dc, hdc. Sc into the next 5 sts.
Ch 2 to create the second corner, then 3 sc. Skip 1, hdc into the middle of the heart peaks (into the 2nd dc from the first rnd of the heart). 3 sc and ch 2 to create the third corner.
5sc, hdc, dc and ch 2 for the fourth and final corner of the granny square.
Dc, hdc,sc and sc again into the first stitch of the rnd where you joined the yarn. Sl st into the first sc through both loops as normal to join. Each side should measure 7 sts long.

Round 3:
Ch 2 (does not count as st), dc into the same st, 2dc. 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc into the corner.
*7 dc across the side, 2 dc ch 2 and 2 dc into the corner. Rep from * 2 more times and complete the 4th corner.
4 dc, sl st into the first dc to join. Each side should now measure 11 sts long.

Round 4:
Ch 2 and dc into the same st, 4 dc. Into the corner make 2 dc, ch 2 and 2 dc altogether (just like the prev. rnd!),
crochet 11 dc across the side. Repeat process until 4th corner is completed, t
crochet 6 dc and sl st into first st to join. Each side should now be 15 dcs long.

Round 5:
Ch 2 and dc into the same st, 7 dc, 2 dc, ch 2 and 2 dc into the corner, 15 dc across the side, repeat process until 4th corner is completed.
8 dc and sl st into first st to join. Ch 1, fasten off and weave in the ends.


STEP 3

Slip Stitch join

hello
Position squares in a checkerboard patterns with right sides facing up
hello
With a slip knot on your hook, insert hook from right side to wrong side into the FRONT LOOP ONLY of the last chain of the working edge of Square
hello
Yarn over and pull through all loops for a slip stitch.
hello
Continue to insert hook into the front loop only of Square A and corresponding back loop only of Square B, yarn over and pull through all loops
hello
This will continue until you reach the first chain of the opposite corner. You will then continue this across another square to connect. This will continue until you have joined all of the squares in that column.


STEP 4

Bag Straps

hello
make 6 sc on the top of the first square and chain 1
hello
flip after finishing each row, and work 6 sc then chain 1
hello
repeat this until you reach your desired length
hello
make another strap on the opposite side
hello
once your done, flipe the bag inside out. You are completely done now!!!
